      Who We Are

      A Proudly Nigerian Energy Company

      W Oil and Gas is dedicated to advancing Nigeria's energy sector through innovation, reliability, and excellence.
      Founded in 2017 and incorporated in Benin City, Edo State with the Corporate Affairs Commission (RC 2537200), W Oil and Gas has quickly grown into a trusted name in Nigeria's oil and gas industry. Under the leadership of our proprietor, Isaiah Esosa Oboite, we have built a company grounded in integrity, professionalism, and a deep commitment to national energy development.
      We operate across the oil and gas value chain — from exploration and production support to petroleum product distribution and energy consulting. Our mission is to contribute meaningfully to Nigeria's energy security while maintaining the highest standards of safety, environmental stewardship, and operational excellence.
